Lasagna

Description

This homemade lasagna recipe is going to be worth every minute! The total time of cooking from prep to finish is three hours and fifteen minutes & will serve 12 people. This lasagna recipe is very simple in its steps! All it takes is to make each component such as the sauce and ricotta mixture, then cooking up the noodles to layer up, foil, then bake! This lasagna requires 50 minutes in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion & garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices & seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta sauce
  4. Layer the lasagna in a repeated order
  5. Cover with foil & bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
